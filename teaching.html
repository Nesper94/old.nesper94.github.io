Teaching

I am convinced that education is the most powerful tool to transform societies, for this reason teaching is a passion and a duty for me.

As part of a scholarship during my MSc I had the opportunity to teach some courses for undergraduate students:

  • General Biology Lab. for Microbiologists
  • Molecular Genetics Lab. for Biologists
  • General Genetics for Biologists

Currently I continue to be assistant professor in the course of General Genetics. Eventually, I also give introductory courses to Linux and Python.
